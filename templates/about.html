About CivicLens

Vision

A city where every civic concern is visible, traceable, and acted on with integrity — transparency between residents and government as the default, not the exception.

Mission

CivicLens equips citizens and departments with evidence-led reporting, crowd verification, and AI-assisted triage so responses are faster, fairer, and accountable — building responsive, AI-enabled governance people can trust.

CivicLens is a smart civic issue reporting system that connects residents, evidence, and departments on one metallic, real-time console. We make public infrastructure responsive, measurable, and fair.

Departments can assign work, upload after photos, and read analytics while citizens follow resolutions and earn recognition for verified contributions.
