About CivicLens

CivicLens is a smart civic issue reporting system that connects residents, evidence, and departments on one metallic, real-time console. Our mission is to make public infrastructure responsive, measurable, and fair.

Departments can assign work, upload after photos, and read analytics while citizens track resolutions and earn recognition for verified contributions.
